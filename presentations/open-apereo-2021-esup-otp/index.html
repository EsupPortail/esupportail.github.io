MFA Providers with Apereo CAS

From CAS v5, MFA works well as CAS Modules !

  • Duo Security
  • Twilio Authy
  • Acceptto
  • YubiKey
  • WiKID
  • FIDO
  • Swivel Secure
  • Google Authenticator
  • ...

But ... most of them are :

  • not open-source
  • expensive
  • not self-hosted

Goal and purpose

ESUP-OTP aims to :
  • be open-source and free : MIT License
  • be self-hosted
  • be easy to use
  • let users choice its MFA
  • let managers help users if needed
